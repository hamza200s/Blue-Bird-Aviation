About Bluebird Aviation

Bluebird Aviation is a leading East African airline specializing in cargo operations, humanitarian relief missions, passenger charters, and medical evacuation flights. With over 25 years of experience, they operate a versatile fleet capable of serving remote and short airstrips across Africa.

  • Founded in Kenya with regional and international operations
  • Expertise in humanitarian relief and emergency response flights
  • Fleet includes Dash-8, CRJ, Beechcraft and Cessna aircraft
  • Trusted by NGOs, governments, and private sector clients

Leadership

Bluebird Aviation is led by a seasoned team of aviation experts with decades of combined experience in airline management, flight operations, logistics, and safety compliance.

Chief Executive Officer

Head of Cargo & Logistics
